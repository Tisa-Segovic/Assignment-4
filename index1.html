After learning that Metropolitan Gala will be hosted by Harry Styles, Selena Williams, LadyGaga and Gucci Alessandro Michele this year, all inspired by extravagance, British Vogue reported that Dior is hosting their show there!

The sensational rivalry of the Paris Fashion Week between Gucci and Dior is obviously continuing. As mentioned, the influential face of Gucci is designing the new Met Gala Show, so Dior is not behind and follows Gucci at every turn. Above all, the largest exhibition of the Dior home ever has been announced in the U.K., in the beautiful Victoria and Albert Museum. According to extensive descriptions from the official V&A Museum pages, this will be an exhibition with a timeline of 1947 until today and will showcase the influence of Dior fashion house with an emphasis on Dior's approach to the U.K.

"There is no other state in the world except my own, whose lifestyle I love so much. I love English tradition, decency, and English architecture. I even like English cuisine," said Christian Dior, the creator od the Dior designs himself.
