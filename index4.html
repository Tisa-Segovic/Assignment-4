And the biggest hit of the cold season is . . .

Recently leading fashion houses mostly no longer use the real fur. I, personally, fully support because there are so many alternatives that look just as attractive. The use of real fur for design purposes is a brutal act that high fashion had to give up sooner or later, and the great Gucci was among the first to reject this trend and turn to some other materials. The last few years faux fur coats enjoy great popularity primarily because of their convenience, comfort and functionality. These seasons are wearing classic black and brown, the standard leopard pattern has been reinforced with other animal specimens, and we love the colors, the same as the pastels.
